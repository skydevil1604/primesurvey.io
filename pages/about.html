PRIMESURVEY  is an independent inspection company rendering comprehensive services in many aspects of cargo superintendence through its offices, representatives, and partner companies. In the Black Sea and the Sea of Azov Basin as well as the CIS countries, we conduct inspection activities with our offices located in Ukraine, Russia and Georgia with a coordinating office in Odessa.

The company was founded as a self-financed organization for the inspection of goods in international commerce. Since the time of its creation, PRIMESURVEY has been rendering comprehensive services of good quality to metals, scrap metal, ores, fertilizers, fats and oils, grain and cereals, sugar and other goods at compatible prices. Our company operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and offers reliable service and flexibility to our customers.

PRIMESURVEY has been recognized by many companies and parties involved in transportation process as an independent and unbiased surveying company. PRIMESURVEY is co-operating with many insurance companies and P&I Clubs in loss prevention and the evaluation of the risks associated with all shipping-related operations.

We perform our inspections in due time to the high standard required to meet such international standards as GAFTA, ISO, FOSFA, GOST and others with issuance of the corresponding certificates. All analytical tests are carried out in the accredited laboratories.

The goal of our company is to protect the interests of our clients and provide accurate, reliable information about the quantity and quality of the products during shipment, handling and storage.

PRIMESURVEY's policy is lying in: giving timely warnings of any shortcomings and abnormalities; decreasing the likelihood of defects; helping to prevent delivery delays; eliminating financial losses or increased expenses caused by non-keeping of contractual terms.

Accurate weights and product quality are just two factors that need to be considered to meet contractual obligations. Our staff that has the necessary knowledge for carrying out any serious and professional inspection is well known by sellers/buyers and colleague inspection companies.

Our inspectors have considerable marine and cargo survey experience and a wide range of technical resources to provide you with a precise, high quality service and assessment. Our Inspectors are fully conversant with internationally accepted sampling and weighing procedures, enabling them to obtain a true reflection of the cargo's quality and final loaded/discharged weight

We provide our clients with a wide range of high quality inspection, quality assurance and related support services through a team of highly trained, diverse and experienced professionals using modern technology.
